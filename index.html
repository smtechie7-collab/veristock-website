Tailored Features for Modern Retail

VeriStock Pro combines powerful ERP capabilities with lightweight mobile operations, helping you manage inventory, accounts, and jobs offline.

Grid Matrix Inventory

Easily track multiple device variants, storage tiers (e.g. 128GB/256GB), color schemes, and batch IMEI numbers in a single grid.

Express Billing

Generate GST-compliant invoices in under three seconds. Compatible with barcode scanners and ESC/POS thermal printers.

GST Invoicing & Reports

Compute CGST, SGST, and IGST components automatically. File tax summaries easily with quick CSV report exports.

Double-Entry Ledger

Keep a strict record of all cash accounts, payment modes, business credits (Udhari), and merchant purchases.

Repair Job Sheets

Register customer repair requests, estimate service milestones, assign diagnostic tasks, and track real-time repair progress.

Intelligent Stock Warnings

Receive notifications for items reaching low stock quantities, helping you optimize and replenish your inventory beforehand.

Offline Local Database

Run completely locally on your device. Never suffer from internet disconnects, cloud failures, or hosting outages.

AES-256 Encryption

Local SQLCipher integration guarantees that all billing records, customer contacts, and prices are fully encrypted at rest.

Secure Cloud Backups

Opt-in to write periodic encrypted backups directly to your personal Google Drive, maintaining full data ownership.

WhatsApp Invoice Sharing

Send GST invoices, repair status updates, and payment reminders to customers via WhatsApp with a single tap — no copy-paste needed.

Biometric & PIN App Lock

Protect your business data with fingerprint biometric or custom PIN lock. Unauthorized access is blocked at the app entry point.

CA-Ready PDF & Excel Export

Export daily, weekly, or monthly sales, stock, and GSTR-1/GSTR-3B reports as PDF or Excel. Share with your Chartered Accountant instantly.
