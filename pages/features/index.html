Core Feature Modules

🧾 Billing & POS LITE

  • GST-compliant invoice generation (CGST, SGST, IGST)
  • Cash, UPI, Card, Split payment modes
  • Barcode scanner support (camera + USB/Bluetooth)
  • ESC/POS 58mm and 80mm thermal printer printing
  • Customer purchase history on every invoice
  • Shop logo, GSTIN, address on receipt header
  • WhatsApp invoice sharing with one tap
  • Quick draft and hold billing flow

📦 Inventory Management LITE

  • Real-time stock tracking across all products
  • Low stock and out-of-stock alerts with notifications
  • Grid Matrix inventory (size × color for garments)
  • Batch and expiry date tracking (pharmacy)
  • IMEI and serial number tracking (mobiles)
  • Product variants, categories, and HSN codes
  • Stock adjustment and movement history
  • Barcode and QR label generation

🔧 Repair Job Management LITE

  • Full job card lifecycle: Intake → Diagnose → Repair → Deliver
  • Technician assignment and deadline tracking
  • Device lock pattern and physical condition notes
  • Estimated parts cost and labour charges
  • WhatsApp repair status update to customer
  • Repair parts deducted from inventory automatically
  • Job search by customer, device, or job ID

🏛️ GST & Tax Compliance LITE

  • Auto-computed GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B summaries
  • HSN code management and summary reports
  • GST rate management per product category
  • Intra-state vs Inter-state tax routing
  • CA-ready export in CSV, PDF, Excel
  • Reverse charge mechanism (RCM) support

💰 Accounting & Ledger LITE

  • Double-entry ledger bookkeeping
  • Customer credit (Udhari) tracking with aging
  • Supplier payables and receivables ledger
  • Expense tracking and profit & loss summary
  • Multiple payment modes (Cash, UPI, Bank, Card)
  • Outstanding payment reminders via WhatsApp
  • Cash in/out journal with daily close

👥 Customer Management LITE

  • Customer profile with contact, address, GSTIN
  • Purchase history and outstanding balance
  • Patient details (Age, Blood Group) for clinic/healthcare
  • KYC records for second-hand mobile buyers
  • Customer-wise ledger with WhatsApp statements

🚛 Suppliers & Purchases LITE

  • Vendor/supplier profiles with GSTIN and bank details
  • Purchase bill entry with GST input credit
  • Purchase return management
  • Supplier payables tracking and aging
  • Stock auto-update on purchase entry

📊 Reports & Analytics LITE

  • Daily, weekly, monthly profit reports
  • Top-selling products and slow-mover analysis
  • Stock valuation and purchase cost reports
  • Export to PDF and Excel — share with CA instantly
  • Advanced analytics and custom date ranges PRO
  • GSTR-1 / GSTR-3B structured export PRO

🔒 Security & Backup LITE

  • AES-256 SQLCipher encrypted local database
  • PIN lock and fingerprint biometric app entry
  • Automatic Google Drive encrypted cloud backup
  • One-tap backup restore on new phone
  • Smart Clone — local device-to-device P2P transfer
  • Data never leaves device without your permission

📱 IMEI & Device Tracking PRO

  • Dual IMEI tracking per device (SIM 1 + SIM 2)
  • IMEI history audit trail (full lifecycle timeline)
  • Used phone buyback KYC and seller ID records
  • IMEI search during billing for instant matching
  • Margin Scheme GST calculations for service repairs
  • Device buyback and trade-in flow connector

🏭 Wholesale Module PRO

  • Dedicated wholesale billing screen (order → invoice)
  • Wholesale price slabs and MOQ management
  • Multi-tier pricing (retail, wholesale, customer-specific)
  • Quotation creation and picker for wholesale orders
  • Bulk discount strategies for large orders

⚙️ Manufacturing Module PRO

  • Bill of Materials (BOM) editor
  • Production Orders management and tracking
  • Production status dashboard widget
  • Serial number tracking for manufactured goods
  • Multi-tier production costing
