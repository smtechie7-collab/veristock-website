Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

1. Introduction

SM Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") builds the VERISTOCK PRO mobile application as a commercial B2B Business ERP utility. This service is intended for retail businesses, shop managers, and service technicians. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our Android application and website.

VeriStock Pro is designed with an Offline-First Architecture. This means all business inventory, sales tickets, bills, customer ledgers, and transaction logs are processed and stored locally on your device rather than on our cloud servers.

2. Permissions and Data We Collect

To deliver advanced local ERP operations, VeriStock Pro requires specific device permissions. These are triggered dynamically and used solely for in-app features:

  • CAMERA: Used to scan barcodes for product catalog management and billing lookup, and to capture photos of device conditions for repair job sheets.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: Used to attach geographic stamps to billing logs and merchant audit trails, ensuring local compliance under Indian business accounting guidelines.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: Used to let merchants upload their business logo, signature, and product listing photos into the local inventory database.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to display in-app alerts for low stock levels, repair delivery milestones, and unpaid ledger credits.
  • BLUETOOTH: Used to establish connections to external 2-inch and 3-inch ESC/POS thermal printers for wireless receipt printing.

3. Data Safety and Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your business data. Your information is treated as follows:

  • Financial and Account Records: Stored locally in a secure database encrypted at rest with SQLCipher (256-bit AES). It remains private to your device.
  • Cloud Authentication (Firebase): Used for secure session management and subscription validation. This details your account username and email only, secured via TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • Google Drive Sync (Opt-In): If you activate the backup utility, the encrypted database file is written directly to your personal Google Drive account. We have no access to this folder.
  • Crash Diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics): Anonymized logs containing system metadata and stack traces are shared to help us debug application errors.

4. Security of Data

We implement industry-standard practices to protect your data. All database structures are encrypted locally. Cloud authentication sessions use standard SSL/TLS encryption. However, because data resides locally, you are responsible for maintaining the physical security of your Android device.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your credentials and cloud authentication data. Under Google Play Store policies, we provide direct self-serve account deletion:

You can delete your cloud-registered authentication records instantly inside the app by heading to Settings -> About -> Delete Account. You can also file a deletion request using our online Account Deletion Form. Cloud authentication credentials will be permanently purged within 7 business days.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any adjustments by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
