Troubleshooting Index

Solve common application configuration challenges quickly:

1. Bluetooth Thermal Printer Discovery Fails

The app queries active Bluetooth channels. If your 58mm/80mm ESC/POS printer does not show up:

  1. Pair the printer device directly in your Android phone's system Bluetooth Settings first (usually using PIN 0000 or 1234).
  2. Check that the printer is turned on and has a full paper roll.
  3. Re-open VeriStock Pro and retry printer search in Settings.

2. Google Drive Cloud Sync Fails

The app backs up encrypted SQLCipher databases to a secure space. If the sync errors:

  • Verify your device has an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Check that your Google Account storage quota is not exceeded.
  • Re-authenticate the Google Drive permission inside the backup panel.

3. Barcode Scanner Camera Permissions

If the barcode scanning viewfinder shows a black screen:

  • Head to Android Settings -> Apps -> VeriStock Pro -> Permissions.
  • Ensure the Camera permission is set to "Allow only while using the app".

4. License Activation Issues

Licenses are managed out-of-band by SM Technologies distribution agents. If you receive an "Invalid Code" warning, verify characters or request a replacement token from your local distributor.
